Write 3 clear and concise prompts for 3 different prompt formats:

1. text prompting - complete text or question.
Ex. You are an expert astrologist. Imagine you are describing a waxing versus a waning moon to a group of high school students. 

2. content generation - create new content given guidelines (instructions) for code, images, etc.
Ex. Write a beginner's guide to artificial intelligence.
Ex. Write a social media post for your bike shop about the latest Trek Top Fuel. 

3. sentiment - analyzing something as positive or negative
Ex. Describe a true moment of happiness.
Ex. Describe a situation where you felt helpless and unable to change the outcome.
